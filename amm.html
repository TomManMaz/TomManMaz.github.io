Problem 12243 - by M. Lawrence Glasser (USA)

For \(a>0\) evaluate \[ I_a = \int_0^a \frac{t}{\sinh t \, \sqrt{1-\frac{\sinh^2(t)}{\sinh^2(a)}}} \mathop{}\!\mathrm{d} t. \]

solution
Problem 12229 - by Moubinool Omarjee (France)

Let \(f \colon [0,1] \to \mathbb R\) be a function that has continuous derivatives and that satisfies \(f(0)=f(1)\) and \(\int_0^1 f(x) \mathop{}\!\mathrm{d}x = 0\). Prove \[ 30240\left(\int_0^1 xf(x) \mathop{}\!\mathrm{d} x\right)^2 \le \int_0^1 \left(f''(x) \right)^2 \mathop{}\!\mathrm{d}x. \]

solution
Problem 12221 by N. Batir (Turkey)

\[ \int_0^1 \frac{\ln(x^6+1)}{x^2+1} \mathop{}\!\mathrm{d} x = \frac{\pi\ln 6 }{2}- 3G, \] where \(G\) is Catalan's constant \(\sum_{n=0}^\infty \frac{(-1)^n}{(2n+1)^2}\).

solution
